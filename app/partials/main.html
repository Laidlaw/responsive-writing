Writing is hard work. Every sentence is a decision to present an idea in a certain light.

When we're having a conversation we can tailor our words, tone and gestures based on the context we are in but writing is meant to work with nearly no context.

The World Wide Web was designed to move words around. Not pictures or sounds - words are the atomic unit of the web. Computer Science has a long history of attempting to link ideas together through the words that represent them.

Since then, the internet has changed how we communicate with one another. We text and tweet but the way we organize our thoughts hasn't changed very much.

Responsive Writing is both an experiment and a conversation. Its a new way to write and new way to connect ideas. Be a part of the conversation.
